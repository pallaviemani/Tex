1. HOW WILL A TEx WORKSHOP HELP MY CHILD?

Our workshops, above all else are designed to be inspiring, thought provoking, creative and innovative where participants, under the guidance of thought leadership and successful professionals, explore who they are and who they want to be. The workshops aim to enable student to think, learn and grow.


2. HOW DO YOU KNOW TEx WORKSHOPS IMPROVE THOUGHT PROCESS?

With limited accessibility of thought leadership who share what they have learnt through their experiences and also through the people they have met .Tex believes it’s important to evaluate our workshops to ensure that they are effective. Recent research has indicated that students who participate in our workshops have an improved sense of life meaning and engagement. Following a TEx workshop, students report enhanced levels of self-awareness and optimism, and the ability to build deeper, more meaningful connections, improved emotional and social skills, and positive wellbeing strategies.


3. WHO ATTENDS TEx WORKSHOPS?

TEx is for all students of class 7 and above.


4. WHO DELIVERS THE WORKSHOPS?

The Thought Leaders who have been successful not only in cracking the toughest examinations but also in their respective professions. The Thought Leaders who design and deliver our workshops have been involved with TEx for over a period of time. They are extensively skilled to create dynamic and interactive spaces for students to share and explore who they are and what is important to them.


5. WHAT WORKSHOPS DOES TEx DELIVER FOR STUDENTS/TEACHERS?

We run workshops for students within schools and in the community. Our community workshops include:
1) Weekend Workshops: a weekend camp usually on Saturday and Sunday depending on the school.Weekends Workshops are open to any student who is Class 7 and above . Depending on the participation the students will be divided into groups.
2) Personalized Workshops:We aim to create holistic and sustainable partnerships with schools. Each workshop is tailored to suit the developmental needs of schools, and issues common to year levels.Personalized Workshops are open to schools who is Class 7 and above . Depending on the participation the students will be divided into groups.
3) Teacher Workshops:TEx offers a unique professional development workshop for teacher called Role Models. It is designed to provide teachers with the skills to deliver best teaching practices in the classroom. The workshop helps participants gain a further understanding the art of teaching (through experience) of Senior Teachers and Professional.
Role Models is a one-day experiential workshop designed and delivered by Thought Leaders at TEx - who have been effectively engaging and inspiring students for several years.


6. WHAT SUPPORT IS AVAILABLE FOR STUDENTS AFTER A WORKSHOP?

TEx provides a safe and supportive environment to all young people who participate in our workshops. We shall conduct pre-workshop assessment. The purpose of this assessment is to test student’s level of understanding. There will be post-workshop assessment which will help us in calculating the progress in the student.The Thought Leaders will be available to provide support and follow up referrals to students.


7. HOW CAN I SUPPORT MY YOUNG PERSON FOLLOWING A TEx WORKSHOP?

TEx workshops provide an opportunity for students to share what is important and meaningful to them. This process of sharing can help a student feel less alone, as they realise that other young people in the group feel the same way. It can also serve as an opportunity to think differently and consider other perspectives.
